How ambient factors affect student's performance


Introduce
Throughout our studying process at school, the grading system was always the main factor that was used to judge the students’ level, ability, or even intelligence. But, does a student’s grade reflect their ability or intelligence. Well, we would say sometimes it does, but not most of the time. This project is created to help visualize and discover trends, and relationships between a student’s performance and their background.
